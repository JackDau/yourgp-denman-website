What Your Day Looks Like as a GP

When you first join, we start you with a reduced patient load — as few as 2-3 patients per hour — so you have time to learn our systems and settle in. We then gradually increase your patient flow as you're ready. Our established GPs typically see 3-4 patients per hour.

Your patients already trust our brand, so about 80% of the trust gap is covered before you meet them. We'll help you create an introduction video so patients can see your personality before they book — this builds rapport before the first consultation.

We provide full administrative and nursing support. Our admin team handles billing, bookings, and correspondence. Our nursing team provides clinical support, patient preparation, and health coaching. You focus on medicine.

What we expect:

  • Clear communication — patients must be able to understand you
  • Clinical quality — excellent clinical decision-making
  • Reliability — consistent attendance and standards
  • Appropriate billing — charging fairly keeps things equitable across the team

We do not currently have capacity for Level 1 Supervision for overseas trained doctors requiring supervision.

Nurses

Our nursing team are integral to the care we provide to patients and GPs. You'll have varied days with a lot of face-to-face patient time, health coaching, and clinical support.

We invest in our team — minimum 20 hours of professional development per year, cross-training opportunities, and a supportive culture where you're valued.

To thrive here, you enjoy helping others with care and excellence, and you're always looking to improve.
