Dr Elizabeth Muller

new patients welcome

ABOUT

Previously practising as a GP in Wanniassa, ACT, Elizabeth has developed experience across a broad range of general practice services — including children’s health, preventive care, chronic disease monitoring and women’s health.

Elizabeth studied medicine at Australian National University (ANU), graduating with distinction in 2016. Following graduation, she completed her internship and residency in Canberra and Goulburn.

Outside of work, Elizabeth enjoys contributing to the health and well-being of her community through general practice. She values compassionate care and continuous learning — striving always to keep up-to-date with best practices so she can provide excellent patient care.

Dr Rachel Liang - Skin

accepting new patients for skin checks

ABOUT

Dr Rachel Liang is a highly experienced specialised skin cancer GP who loves her work and
helping her patients. She is passionate about skin cancer diagnosis and surgery and has
been working solely in this field for over a decade.

Dr Rachel Liang is a holistic and empathetic practitioner who is committed to achieving
excellent outcomes for her patients. She has many years of surgical experience and has
undertaken extensive surgical training from a leading plastic surgeon.

Dr Rachel Liang completed her medical degree at the University of Otago in 2006. She
obtained certification in Skin Cancer Medicine and Surgery with the University of
Queensland in 2013. She is a fellow of the Royal Australian College of General Practitioners
and is completing her Master of Medicine Degree in Skin Cancer with the University of
Queensland.

Outside of work, Dr Rachel Liang delights in reading, exercising and whipping up culinary
delights for her family. Her favourite days are ones spent having fun with her husband and
two darling boys.

Dr Chi So - Skin

accepting new patients for skin checks

ABOUT

Dr Chi So specialises in skin cancer diagnosis and management. He believes in an empathetic and caring approach to his consultations and procedures and is able to perform full body skin checks, mole and spot checks, dermoscopy, skin cancer surveillance, skin cancer excisions, biopsies, and minor procedures.

He completed the requirements for a Fellowship of the Royal College of General Practitioners in 2017. He went on to complete a Master in Medicine (Skin Cancer) and a Master of Public Health.

He has published research papers in international journals exploring the field of skin cancer management in Australian primary care.

He says he enjoys developing trusting relationships with his clients over many years as he provides care.

Dr Anna Colwell - travel medicine consults

new patients for travel only

ABOUT

Anna completed Medicine at the University of Tasmania. Since then she has practiced predominately in Canberra but also in rural, remote and overseas places. She has had further training and experience in public health, infectious diseases and travel medicine, including obtaining a Masters of Public Health and Tropical Medicine, and Fellowship with the Australasian College of Tropical Medicine. Anna has worked for the Commonwealth Government in relation to immunisation, outbreaks, disaster planning and veteran’s health and was a member of the ACT Health COVID Response Team. She has interests in lifestyle medicine, infectious diseases and travel health.

Outside of work Anna enjoys hikes and rides with family and friends, reading, growing vegetables and creating cakes when there is a special event.
